### Truly a Summer to Remember

I learned many lessons and developed many friendships alongside the privilege to learn many skills and talents. From state-of-the-art presentations on rotorcraft, to implementing my own scripts and algorithms for database management — I loved this job.

---

## What is the MV-75?

The MV-75 is the winner of the **Future Long Range Assault Aircraft (FLRAA)** program — designed as a replacement to the time-tested UH-60 Black Hawk.

The Black Hawk has long served as a foundational utility vehicle for the military, offering vertical lift in an easily configurable package. As my coworkers put it: *helicopters are the Jeeps of the sky* — they flex to many roles and accomplish them well. However, their most notable limitation is **range**.

Bell's solution — the **tilt-rotor** — seeks to provide the same level of utility while overcoming that limiting factor. In short, the MV-75 delivers Black Hawk-level versatility with dramatically extended range.

The program was clearly a success: the United States Army committed a jaw-dropping **$70 billion** to it, making it **the largest acquisition in Army history**.

## Experiences & Highlights

Bell — and by extension Textron — made it clear from day one that they are earnest about investing in new talent. They provided dozens of resources and opportunities for emerging professionals to learn and grow.

Among the experiences I had the privilege of participating in:

- **3DX, CATIA & PLM Training** — Industry-grade lessons where we designed parts in accordance with company modeling practices and intellectual property standards.
- **Rotorcraft & Tiltrotor Lectures** — State-of-the-art presentations covering the underlying physical principles and design decisions across various aircraft systems.
- **Engineering Data Management** — Analyzed, sanitized, and implemented engineering datasets into production databases.
- **Automation & Scripting** — Designed scripts and algorithms to automate 3DX configuration management, improving task efficiency by **99.54%**.
- **Government Interface** — Interfaced with the United States Government and Department of Defense on a weekly basis through a variety of meetings.
- **Facility Tours** — Toured 3+ state-of-the-art production facilities, gaining firsthand exposure to production and machining technologies.
- **Industry Networking** — Regularly networked with subject matter experts and technical competency leads for firsthand feedback on ideas and theories.
